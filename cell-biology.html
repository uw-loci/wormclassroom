Cell Biology Research Questions

Cell biologists study phenomena inside a cell. They ask questions such as: how do different cellular components interact with each other during cell division, how do microtubules (MTs) form, what molecules are MTs composed of and how do MTs catch and move chromosomes to the right place at the right time.

Here is a list of C. elegans cell biology research questions to get you to start thinking about research in cell biology.
